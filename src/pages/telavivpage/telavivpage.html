THINGS TO DO IN & AROUND TEL AVIV

Tel Aviv is one of the most vibrant cities in the world. The New York Times named Tel Aviv, "The Mediterranean Capital of Cool." Tel Aviv is a 24-hour city with a unique heartbeat, combining sandy beaches, world-class nightlife, a broad cultural scene, and incredible food.
Please find below a list of suggestions that include restaurants, sightseeing, and more. We also have a 24-hour Ayelet Tours Host who is available to assist with recommendations and reservations.
*Note: When touring, especially in the markets, it is important to keep a close eye on your belongings. While theft is not common in Tel Aviv, it is important to be cautious.

MUSEUMS

Tel Aviv Museum of Art:
Tel Aviv Museum of Art is a municipal museum, one of Israel's leading artistic and cultural institutions. The museum comprises various departments including the Department of Israeli Art, the Department of Modern and Contemporary Art, the Department of Architecture and Design, and more!

Beit Hatfutsot, Museum of the Jewish People:
Beit Hatfutsot is a unique global institution that tells the story of the Jewish people as it connects Jewish people to their roots and strengthens their personal and collective Jewish identity. Beit Hatfutsot conveys to the world the fascinating narrative of the Jewish people and the essence of the Jewish culture, faith, purpose and deed while presenting the contribution of world Jewry to humanity.

Bauhaus Museum:
This is a boutique gallery for those who love architecture; it is a one-room museum within an original Bauhaus building.
